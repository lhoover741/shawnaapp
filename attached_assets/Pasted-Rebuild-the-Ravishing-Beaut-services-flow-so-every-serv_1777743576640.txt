Rebuild the Ravishing Beauté services flow so every service card is tappable.

Main goal:
When a client taps a service on the Services page, open a dedicated service detail/booking page for that exact service. That page should let them review the service, select applicable add-ons, see an estimated total, then tap a booking/text button that opens their text messages with a prefilled booking request including everything they selected.

Required behavior:
1. Every service card on the Services page must be clickable/tappable.
2. Clicking a service opens a service detail page or route, such as:
   /service/small-knotless-braids
   /service/medium-knotless-braids
   /service/large-knotless-braids
   /service/feed-in-braids
   /service/stitch-braids
   /service/bob-braids
   /service/sleek-ponytail
   /service/quick-weave
3. Each service detail page should include:
   - Service name
   - Starting price
   - Estimated duration
   - Short description
   - Hair inclusion note when applicable
   - Deposit note
   - Add-on selector with checkboxes/toggles
   - Estimated total calculation
   - “Text to Book” button
4. The Text to Book button must open the client’s SMS/messages app with a prefilled message.
5. The prefilled message must include:
   - Selected service
   - Starting price
   - Selected add-ons
   - Estimated total
   - Deposit required: $25
   - Preferred date
   - Preferred time
   - Client name
   - Hair color selection if braiding hair is included
6. Keep the design mobile-first, polished, feminine, clean, and premium.
7. Keep the bottom nav working.
8. Do not use Instagram embeds.
9. Keep Ravishing Beauté branding consistent.

Important business rules:
- $25 non-refundable deposit required.
- Business hours are 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM by appointment.
- Closed Sunday and Monday.
- Same-day bookings only if approved.
- Please come detangled to stay on schedule.
- Braiding hair is included only in natural colors: 1, 1B, 2, and 4.
- Do not say detangling is free.
